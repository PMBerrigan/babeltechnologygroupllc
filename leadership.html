Patrick Berrigan

President

With over 20 years of service in the information technology field spanning the Department of Defense and Intelligence Community, Patrick brings deep operational expertise to every engagement. His experience spans system engineering, cybersecurity, risk management, and cloud infrastructure across multiple defense agencies including the Defense Intelligence Agency, National Media Exploitation Center, and all branches of the U.S. military.

Patrick founded Babel Technology Group with a mission to make enterprise-grade IT consulting accessible to organizations of all sizes. Under his leadership, the company developed Babel Bastion — an AI-driven CMMC compliance platform designed to help small and mid-size defense contractors navigate the complex path to certification without the typical six-figure consulting fees.
